ABOUT

PT. CMA INDONESIA (Construction Machine Automation). berdiri sejak tahun 1995 sampai sekarang bergerak di bidang Kontruksi gedung, Operator Kontol Mesin Otomatis, Perawatan Maintenance Servis, Saat ini Kami telah memiliki kurang lebih 1500 Tenaga Pekerja yang bersertifikasi khusus Teknisi handal di seluruh indonesia, sudah berkerja sama sebagai mitra di seluruh project Kontruksi, mesin dan Perawatan diwilayah kerja project Swasta maupun Negeri

CMA INDONESIA COMPANY (Construction Machine Automation). established since 1995 until now engaged in the field of building construction, automatic machine control operators, maintenance maintenance services, we currently have approximately 1500 workers who are certified as reliable technicians throughout Indonesia, have worked together as partners in all construction projects, machinery and maintenance in the work area of private and public projects.
